My name is Kabir Gupta. I'm born on 26th September 2009 And I come from India. I started News 11 as a way for my friends to keep up with news, without having to watch boring news channels or read most wordy and incomprehensible Newspapers. I am only hiring children (like me) so that the newspaper isn't too wordy. I also wanted to make it so that it has a Childlike sense of humor. There are Memes for people who only like to look at pictures, and Comics for readers who like to look at pictures. Its also a way to keep up with everything that has been happening even if you have been sick. That's the story of why I started News 11
